E-Commerce Sales Dashboard(Excel/Power BI)
Power BIExcelDAX
Overview

Build an Power BI dashboards to analyze 3.5 years of retail sales data using Excel and Power BI. Excel was used for data cleaning and preprocessing and develop KPIs, while Power BI was used to create visualization to identify trends, regional performance and product-level insights. it was completed as my first project at Infotact Solutions.

Problem Statement

The Company collects large volumes of retail sales data, but the raw data does not provide clear insights into trends, product performance or revenue growth. Inconsistent time periods furhter compliance performance analysis. This Project aims to transform the data into meaningful visual insights to support better business decisions.

Problem Objective

The objective of this project is to clean and preprocess sales data, perform analysis to identify key metrics such as monthly and yearly trends and evaluate product performance. It also compares partial years data (6 months) with previous years to assess potential profit or loss. the final goal is to develop interactive dashboards in excel and Power BI to deliver actionable insights for decision making.

Steps in Process
Collected and workded with retail product sales data from excel spanning multiple periods.
Cleaned and transformed raw data by removing duplicates, handling missing values and standardizing formats.
Structured the cleaned data into organized tables to enable efficient analysis and reporting.
Developed Key performance indicators (KPIs) including total sales, monthly and yearly trends and core business metrics.
Built interactive dashboards in Excel and Power BI using effective visualizations to communicate insgihts clearly.
What I Did
Designed 6 interactive report pages covering Monthly Sales, product, Total Sales by Year, Sales by Regions & Products, Yearly Sales Performance in Individual and Product Sales Data Analysis and Dashboard.
Develop KPI to measure Sales performance including revenue trends, product performance and regional contribution and Salesperson trends efficiency.
Identified key trends in retail sales: 2024 grew 4.4% over 2023. In the frst half, 2024 increased by 0.96%, while 2025 shows a slight decline of 0.2% indicating slower early growth. Final 2025 revenue will depend on second half performance and cost management.
Highlighed top-performing products contributing the highest revenue.
Outcome
The dashboard reduced hypothetical reporting effort significantly and gave stakeholders real-time visibility into sales performance without needing to open a single spreadsheet.

Customer Churn Prediction
Machine Learning
2024
Pythonscikit-learnPandasSeaborn
Overview

Used a real-world telecom dataset to build a churn prediction model that identifies customers likely to cancel their subscription. The project covered the full pipeline from raw data to model evaluation.

Problem Statement

A telecom company was losing subscribers without knowing which customers were at risk. Without a predictive model, the retention team had no way to proactively intervene before customers churned.

Problem Objective

Build a machine learning classifier that accurately predicts which customers are likely to churn, so the business can target them with retention campaigns before they leave.

Steps in Process
Explored 7,000+ customer records to understand feature distributions, churn rate, and class imbalance.
Handled nulls, encoded categorical variables, and normalized numerical features for model training.
Trained and compared Logistic Regression and Random Forest classifiers using cross-validation.
Assessed models using precision, recall, F1 score, ROC-AUC, and confusion matrices.
Identified top churn drivers via feature importance to derive business recommendations.
What I Did
🧹
Cleaned and preprocessed 7,000+ customer records — handled nulls, encoded categoricals, and normalized features.
🤖
Trained Logistic Regression and Random Forest classifiers and compared performance using precision, recall, and F1 score.
📈
Built visual reports with confusion matrices, ROC curves, and feature importance charts using matplotlib and seaborn.
💡
Found key churn drivers — contract type, tenure, and monthly charges were the strongest predictors of churn.
Outcome
Delivered a clear, well-documented notebook with actionable recommendations for reducing churn by targeting high-risk customer segments.

COVID-19 Trend Analysis
Public Data Analysis
2023
SQLTableauPython
Overview

Analyzed publicly available COVID-19 datasets from WHO and Our World in Data to track how different countries managed the pandemic across waves, vaccination rollouts, and mortality rates.

Problem Statement

Publicly available COVID-19 data was scattered across multiple sources and formats, making it difficult to draw meaningful cross-country comparisons on how vaccination and policy responses affected mortality outcomes.

Problem Objective

Consolidate, clean, and visualize multi-country COVID-19 data into a single interactive dashboard that clearly shows the relationship between vaccination rollout speed and case fatality rates across different waves.

Steps in Process
Pulled datasets from WHO and Our World in Data covering 50+ countries across multiple pandemic waves.
Wrote SQL joins and aggregations to merge case, death, and vaccination tables into a unified dataset.
Used Pandas to fix date formats, standardize country codes, and compute 7-day rolling averages.
Built choropleth maps, wave timelines, and vaccination vs. fatality rate comparison charts in Tableau.
Identified key insights around early vaccination adoption and its impact on later wave mortality.
What I Did
🗄
Wrote SQL queries to extract, join, and aggregate data across 50+ countries from multiple source tables.
🐍
Used Python (Pandas) to clean date formats, handle inconsistent country codes, and compute rolling averages.
🗺
Built a Tableau dashboard with choropleth maps, wave timelines, and vaccination vs. fatality rate comparisons.
📌
Identified key patterns — countries with early vaccination drives saw significantly lower case fatality rates in later waves.
Outcome
Produced a fully interactive Tableau story that made complex pandemic data accessible and comparable across 50+ nations.

College Placement Study
Academic Research
2023
ExcelPythonStatistics
Overview

Final-year academic project analyzing placement outcomes for 500+ students at Loyola College. The study aimed to identify which factors most strongly predicted whether a student would get placed and at what salary.

Problem Statement

The college lacked data-driven insight into what actually drives student placement. Career counselors were offering generic advice without knowing which measurable factors — CGPA, skills, or internships — genuinely predicted placement success.

Problem Objective

Analyse placement records of 500+ students to statistically identify the strongest predictors of placement success and salary outcome, and present findings as actionable curriculum recommendations to the faculty panel.

Steps in Process
Gathered survey and placement data from 500+ students across departments via structured forms.
Removed incomplete entries, standardized categorical variables, and validated numerical ranges in Excel and Python.
Used descriptive statistics and pivot charts to identify patterns across departments and demographics.
Applied chi-square tests, correlation analysis, and regression to validate which factors were statistically significant.
Compiled findings into a faculty-ready dashboard and report with clear, evidence-based curriculum recommendations.
What I Did
📋
Collected and cleaned survey data from 500+ students across departments covering CGPA, internships, skills, and placement outcomes.
📐
Applied statistical tests — chi-square, correlation analysis, and regression to identify significant predictors of placement.
📊
Visualized findings in Excel with pivot charts, scatter plots, and a summary dashboard presented to the faculty panel.
🔑
Key finding: Internship experience was a stronger predictor of placement than CGPA alone across all departments.
Outcome
The report was submitted as the final-year project and presented to a faculty panel. Findings were used to recommend curriculum changes encouraging internship participation.
